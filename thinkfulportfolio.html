About Me

As a beginner in coding, I am still learning on a daily basis. Sometimes it can get overwhelming with the amount of languages and concepts to learn. However, it didn't take long to find out that coding is so much fun! Solving problems and having a sense of achievement can get quite addicting.

Outside of coding and school, I like to de-stress by walking and playing with my dog, Tofu (yes, he is the handsome dog in the picture). My love for animals started young. I grew up in Arizona and was surrounded by horses throughout my childhood. I fell in love with horseback riding and so did my grade-school friends. My horse-crazy classmates and I started playing a browser-based multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) called Horseisle. I am both embarrassed and proud to admit that my squad were the weird horse-obsessed kids in elementary school. Besides the point, Horseisle was the first game that introduced coding to kids. It is crazy that the game had us code our profiles in HTML without even knowing it! I guess I can say I started coding when I was 10 years old. Now, everytime I code in HTML, I get a wonderful nostalgic feeling.
